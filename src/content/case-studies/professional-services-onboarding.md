## The Challenge

A consulting firm with 15 employees was drowning in administrative work. Client intake forms were processed manually, meeting scheduling required back-and-forth emails, weekly reports were assembled by hand, and no one had a clear picture of project status without asking around.

## Our Approach

We implemented a multi-phase AI onboarding:

1. **Client Intake Automation** — New inquiry forms are processed by AI, key data is extracted and populated into their CRM, and an intake summary is generated automatically
2. **Scheduling Automation** — AI coordinates meeting times by reading availability and sending calendar invites, eliminating the email ping-pong
3. **Report Generation** — Weekly client reports are auto-generated from project management data, requiring only a quick human review before sending
4. **Status Dashboard** — A lightweight dashboard aggregates project status from multiple tools, giving leadership real-time visibility

## The Results

- **20 hours/week saved** across the team on administrative tasks
- Client onboarding time reduced from 3 days to same-day
- Weekly report preparation dropped from 4 hours to 30 minutes
- Team morale improved as employees focused on client work instead of admin
- The firm took on 3 new clients without adding headcount
